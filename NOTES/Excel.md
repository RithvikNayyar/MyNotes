###### **Steps to Create "mmm-yyyy" Date Format in Excel**
1. **Select Cells**:
    - Highlight the cells containing the dates.
2. **Open Format Cells**:
    - Right-click and choose **Format Cells** OR use:
        - **Home tab → Number → More Number Formats**.
3. **Navigate to Custom Format**:
    - In the **Format Cells** dialog box:
        - Select **Number tab**.
        - Choose **Custom** from the list.
4. **Enter Custom Code**:
    - Type `mmm-yyyy` in the **Type:** field.
5. **Apply Changes**:
    - Click **OK** to confirm. Your dates will now appear as "Jan-2025".
